"The absence of my sanity was born somewhere."

— PHILLIP PATTERSON

A digital exhibition of
material tension.

My practice exists at the intersection of fragility and force. I work with the physical properties of matter — the way glass fractures under tension, how pigment cracks and blooms across a surface, how light moves through layered resin.

Each piece is an argument between control and surrender. The works do not depict emotion; they embody its residue.

THE STUDIO

Material is the message.

I do not illustrate texture. I engineer it. Fumed silica creates micro-fractures that catch light like frost. Resin pools and magnifies. Glass microspheres float like suspended dust in a sunbeam.
